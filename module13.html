Module 13 Layout - Lost Pet Poster

This module focuses on creating a visual layout for a lost pet poster. The design emphasizes clear hierarchy, strong typography, and visual balance to ensure the information is easy to read and attention-grabbing. The poster includes a prominent title, an image of the pet, descriptive details, and contact information so that viewers can quickly understand the purpose and take action.
